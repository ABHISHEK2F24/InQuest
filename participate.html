InQuest : Participate from home

The teams which are not selected to work in InQuest 2.0, do not get dissssss! Team InQuest brings you "InQuest : Participate from home"

The instructions for the contest are as follows:

  • 1. The teams will have to study their first preference from the research papers provided and will have to submit a review article before
    11 P.M. on 11th November 2017.
  • 2. The research paper will be made available on the website on 11th November, 2017 at 07:30 A.M.
  • 3. The review article submitted should have no plagarism. Any plagarised review article will be disqualified.
  • 4. All the sources and references should be mentioned at the end of the review article in the "References" section.
  • 5. The review article should be submitted at svnit.scosh@gmail.com.
  • 6. The review articles must be in PDF format and must contain atleast 3 page content (excluding title and references). The typesetting must be done in 12pt font with 1.5pt vertical spacing between lines with A4 page normal margin. The headings (if any) can be typeset in 14pt font.
  • 7. Only team participation will be considered. All the details of the teams should be mailed to svnit.scosh@gmail.com along with the review article.
  • 8. Winners will be decided according to quality, depth, coverage and originality of work.
  • 9. Only those teams registered in InQuest 2.0 are allowed to participate.
  • 10. The decision of the committee regarding winner shall be final and no queries will be entertained later.
