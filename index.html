About

InQuest is an initiative to nurture research aptitude in the minds of students. Its first of its kind event where participants rigorously work to study a topic and express their mind driven intutive picture of the same. It aims to foster the growth of research as a community task where people can imagine innovate and inspire.

In InQuest 2016 participants will probe into a topic of their choice. At the end of the given time they have to present their understanding of the concepts in the most original manner possible. One who investigates the topic with the entire coverage and appropriate depth will lift the winner's cup.

FAQ

Who can attent the event?

All the enthusiastic minds who want to understand the working of the nature can participate irrespective of their year or branch.

What do I need to bring on the day of the event?

A curious mind ? Laptops and your favourite books and your coffee mug could be handy!.

How much does it cost?

After you get selected you have to pay 50 rupees as participation fee.

What if I don't have a team?

Keep calm and we will sort it out. We’ll have team formation on the day of the event or before.

What do i get by attending InQuest?

Goodies. Tshirts. Swags. That’s it ? NO ! You get a truckload of knowledge ! That’s what defines InQuest. We will make sure you had 10 hours of real life scientist feel.
