About

InQuest is an initiative to nurture research aptitude in the minds of students. It is a first of its kind event where participants get a glimpse of collaborative research environment. In its first edition, participants rigorously worked on a topic and presented their understanding of the concepts in the most original way possible.

In InQuest 2.0 participants will be given research papers to work on. At the end, they have to express their mind driven intuitive picture of the ideas presented in the research paper.

FAQs

Who can attend the event?

All the enthusiastic minds who want to understand the working of nature can participate.

What do I need to bring on the day of the event?

A curious mind! Laptops, your favourite books and your coffee mug could be handy.

How much does it cost?

Registration is free. After you get selected, you have to pay Rs. 50/- per head as participation fee.

What if I don't have a team?

Keep calm and we will sort it out. We will aid you in team formation.

What do I get by attending InQuest?

Goodies. Tshirts. Swags. That’s it ? NO ! You get a truckload of knowledge ! That’s what defines InQuest. We will make sure you had 10 hours of real life scientist feel.

Can we apply as a team? How can a team be?

Yes! InQuest is strictly a team competition. We believe that you’re stronger as a team than you are apart. You can join or create a team once you've started an individual application but preference will be provided to those who have been registered as a team. You can form teams of up to 3 people.

How does selection process works?

More preference will be given to teams of 3, followed by 2 and then individual participation. In case of same number of team or dispute judging criteria will be write-ups, which is in registration form.

What am I supposed to do in InQuest 2.0 once I get selected?

During the event, we will provide you research paper according to your order of preference. You need to study the paper and cover as much depth as you are comfortable with. At the end of 10 hours, you will be required to present your ideas and understanding of the research paper. Till then you can relax. .
