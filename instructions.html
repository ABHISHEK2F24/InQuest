The rules for the event are as follows :

The participants need to register in teams of maximum 3 individuals. They can also participate (register) as individual or in a team of 2.

More preference (for selection) will be given to teams of 3, followed by 2 and then individual participation.

Participants will be given a pool of research papers, out of which theyâ€™ll have to select minimum 3 in a preference order.

There are some research papers in each pool reserved exclusively for teams having all participants from 1st and 2nd year participants.

The participants will be asked a few questions before finishing the form. They will be ranked on the basis of their answers to the given questions.

The selected participants will be given a research paper based on their preference order.

In case of two teams having a preference dispute their ranking (see rule 5) will be considered and the team with higher preference will be selected first.

In case of team ranking and paper assigning, decision of the committee stands final. No complaints will be entertained in this matter.

Any queries regarding the event should be mailed to admin@scosh.co.in
